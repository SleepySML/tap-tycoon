Response Time

We aim to respond to all messages within 2–3 business days. During busy periods — such as after a major game update — response times may be slightly longer. We appreciate your patience and promise to get back to every genuine inquiry.

If you are reporting a critical bug that is preventing you from playing, please include your browser name and version, your operating system, and a brief description of what happened and what you expected to happen. Screenshots are always helpful if you can include them.
