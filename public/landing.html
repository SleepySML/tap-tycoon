What Is Basement Tycoon?

Everything you need to know about the game before you start playing

A Free Idle Clicker Browser Game

Basement Tycoon is a free, casual idle clicker game that runs directly in your web browser — no download, no app store, no installation required. You start as a humble entrepreneur with a single lemonade stand and build your way up to owning banks and oil companies.

The game blends two proven genres: the satisfying instant feedback of clicker games with the long-term strategy of idle tycoon simulations. Tap to earn money in the beginning, then gradually step back as your passive income does the work for you.

Basement Tycoon is completely free to play. There are no in-app purchases, no pay-to-win mechanics, and no premium currency walls. Every feature in the game is accessible to every player from the very first moment.

Build Your Business Empire

The core loop is simple and deeply satisfying: earn money by tapping, spend it on businesses that generate passive income, then reinvest that income into upgrades that multiply your earnings. Over time, your income per second grows from a few cents to millions of dollars — all without lifting a finger.

The prestige system adds a layer of long-term strategy. Once your total earnings reach a milestone, you can reset your businesses in exchange for a permanent multiplier that makes every future run faster and more profitable. Each prestige is a fresh start that feels more powerful than the last.

With 10 businesses to unlock, 50+ upgrades to discover, and dozens of achievements to earn, there is always a new goal to work toward — whether you have five minutes or five hours to play.

How to Play

Get up and running in minutes with this quick guide

Step 1 — Start Tapping

Create a free account using your Google account or email and password, then jump straight into the game. You will land on the main screen with the tap button front and center. Every tap earns you money — start clicking to fund your first investment.

In the early game, tapping is your primary income source. Each tap earns a base amount that grows over time as you unlock tap-boosting upgrades. The more you invest in tap upgrades, the more valuable each click becomes.

Step 2 — Buy Your First Business

Head to the Business tab and purchase your first Lemonade Stand as soon as you have enough cash. Businesses generate income automatically every second without any tapping required. The more businesses you own and the higher their level, the more passive income you earn.

Each time you buy an additional copy of the same business, its income output increases. Owning 10, 25, 50, or 100 units of a business triggers special milestone bonuses that dramatically boost its earning potential.

Step 3 — Unlock Upgrades

The Upgrades tab contains powerful one-time purchases that permanently enhance your earnings. Upgrades multiply tap income, boost specific businesses, reduce purchase costs, and provide global income multipliers. Prioritize upgrades that affect businesses you already own heavily.

As you grow richer, upgrades become increasingly impactful. A single late-game upgrade can double or triple your entire passive income. Keep an eye on the Upgrades tab and purchase new ones as they become available — they are almost always worth it.

Step 4 — Prestige and Repeat

Once you have earned enough total money, the Prestige tab becomes available. Prestiging resets your businesses and cash in exchange for Prestige Points, which provide a permanent percentage bonus to all income. Your next run starts from scratch, but with a multiplier that makes everything happen much faster.

Experienced players often find that prestiging strategically — rather than waiting as long as possible — is the fastest path to massive long-term wealth. Each reset is a new opportunity to apply everything you have learned.

Game Rules

How the game works under the hood

  • Tap the money button to earn cash equal to your current tap value.
  • Each business generates income automatically every second while the game is open.
  • Businesses cost 15% more each time you purchase another copy of them.
  • Reaching 10, 25, 50, or 100 units of a business triggers a production milestone bonus.
  • Upgrades are permanent and persist through prestige resets.
  • Offline earnings are calculated at 50% of your online income rate, up to 8 hours.
  • The Boost reward doubles all income for 2 hours after watching a short advertisement.
  • Prestige becomes available once your all-time earnings reach the prestige threshold.
  • Each Prestige Point adds a permanent 5% bonus multiplier to all income sources.
  • Achievements reward you with cash bonuses when you hit specific milestones.
  • Game progress is saved to the cloud automatically every few minutes when signed in.
  • All in-game currency and earnings are fictional and have no real-world monetary value.

Tips for Players

Advice from experienced tycoons to help you grow faster

Tip 01

Buy Upgrades Early

Check the Upgrades tab often. Upgrades provide multiplicative bonuses that compound with your business income. Buying an upgrade that doubles a business is almost always better than buying one more unit of that business.

Tip 02

Hit Milestone Quantities

Owning 10, 25, 50, or 100 units of a business triggers special milestone multipliers. Try to push businesses to these thresholds — the jump in income is significant and worth the investment.

Tip 03

Prestige Early and Often

You do not need to wait until you have maxed every business before prestiging. Each prestige gives you a permanent multiplier — the sooner you start stacking those multipliers, the faster you grow overall.

Tip 04

Use the Boost Reward

The 2× income boost lasts a full 2 hours and is completely free. Activate it whenever you plan to spend time tapping actively — during a boost session you can earn as much as you would normally earn in four hours.

Tip 05

Log In Daily

Daily login bonuses and streak rewards add up to significant income over time. Even if you only have a minute to spare, logging in to collect your offline earnings and claim your daily bonus is always worth it.

Tip 06

Spread Your Investment

Rather than maxing out a single business, spread your purchases across several businesses. Diversification gives you more passive income streams and unlocks more upgrades, which provides better long-term returns than focusing on one source.
