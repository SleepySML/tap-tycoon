Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tap Tycoon ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our web application at 500-dollar-game.vercel.app (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the Service:

  • Account data: email address and password hash when you register with email, or your Google/Telegram account identifier when you use OAuth sign-in.
  • Game progress data: in-game state (businesses owned, currency balance, upgrades, achievements) stored in our database so your progress syncs across devices.
  • Usage data: basic analytics such as session duration and feature interactions, collected to improve the game.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To save and sync your game progress.
  • To improve the Service.
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense.

3. Google AdSense and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to show you personalized ads based on your browsing activity across sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info .

Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by Google's Privacy Policy .

4. Cookies

We use cookies and local storage to keep you signed in and to save your game session. Third-party advertising partners (Google AdSense) may also set cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the Service may not work correctly.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account and game data are stored in Supabase, a cloud database service. We take reasonable measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication and database (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdSense — advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In — OAuth authentication (Privacy Policy)

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
