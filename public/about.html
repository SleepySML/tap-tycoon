About the Game

Basement Tycoon is a free, browser-based casual idle game where players build a business empire from scratch. Starting with nothing more than a humble lemonade stand, you tap to earn your first dollars, then reinvest those earnings into an ever-growing portfolio of businesses — from pizza shops and car washes to movie studios, banks, and oil companies.

Unlike traditional clicker games that require constant attention, Basement Tycoon features a robust passive income system. Your businesses continue generating revenue even when you step away from the screen, so you always return to a bigger fortune. The game is designed to be fun for a few minutes at a time or hours at a stretch — it fits any play style.

Basement Tycoon runs entirely in your web browser on desktop and mobile. There is no app to install, no account required to explore, and absolutely no cost to play. Your progress is saved to the cloud and synced across all your devices automatically once you sign in.

Features

Basement Tycoon is packed with features that keep the gameplay engaging and rewarding whether you are a casual player checking in once a day or a dedicated grinder chasing top scores. Here is what you can expect when you start playing:

  • 10 unique businesses to buy and level up
  • 50+ upgrades to unlock and stack
  • Prestige system with permanent multipliers
  • 50+ achievements with cash rewards
  • Cloud save — sync across all devices
  • Offline earnings — earn while away
  • Boost rewards — 2× income for 2 hours
  • Daily login bonuses and streak rewards
  • Fully responsive — desktop and mobile
  • Google and email sign-in options

The prestige system is one of the most satisfying features. Once your total earnings reach a certain threshold, you can reset your businesses in exchange for a permanent income multiplier. Each prestige run is faster and more lucrative than the last, creating a deeply satisfying progression loop that keeps players coming back.
